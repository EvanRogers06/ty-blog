This a blog about my expierience during the TY program at Red Hat
I will be recording the events that take place during the week and what I think of it

ðŸ”± Day one ðŸ”±

@EvanR March 21,2023
Once I entered the building of Red Hat, I reacheda receptionarea and was greeted by a woman named Becky, I was sent to desk where we were given a laptop, a keycard and a bag of various gifts such as a water bottle and a speaker.

We were then escorted out into a conference room, where we were intoduced to some members of staff, and told eachother what schools we were from, after this we were given icebreaker games to do, such as one where each of us had a famous persons name stuck to our back and we had to ask people questions on who we had, we then were given a roleplay to do about how Red Hat makes its money

We then were told about what we would be doing for the week

We were then given a tour of the building which was given to us from becky, then we were allowed to have our lunch. Upon us finishing lunch,we had returned to the conference room and we were given a talk on how to use the command line on linux,and how to use git and github, which was given to us by two members of staff called Fiona and Thomas , we were then tasked with creating a blog on how our week at Red Hat went,with us needing to update it daily in accordance with any events or workshops that take place over our time.

ðŸ”±Day Two ðŸ”±

On day two we were given time at the beggining of the day to be able to continue work on our blogs as we had to make more progress on them to be able to complete them, after spending a while on the graphic design of this blog, we were given a talk on the agile work process, where it was shown to us how to work efficiently in a team and how to constantly improve our skills. We then were given more time to work on our blog, and later in the day we were given a workshop on microbits and python which was given to us by Dominik.

EvanR March 22, 2023

Day three began with us continuing to work on our blog. We then went to the conference room and were tasked with assembling a lego set of an Formula One racing car, which we did not manage to complete, after that though we given a talk about what it is like to work as a software engineer which was given to us by Rachel Lawton,and we then had lunch, after that we were given a talk on student life studying computer science by Daniel Lawton, and after completing that we finished making the lego car.

ðŸ”± Day Four ðŸ”±

On the fourth day we started with us working on our blog for a few minutes, after that we were sent into a meeting where we introduced ourselves to everyone, and where we told everyone one thing about ourself, we then listened to various annoucments about things that would be taking place,we then had breakfast, then at 11 we had a talk about how you can link your passions to our work, with the example of mapping of formula one data with various types of software, we then went back to working on our blog for a short while
