This a blog about my expierience during the TY program at Red Hat
I will be recording the events that take place during the week and what I think of it

Day one

@EvanR March 21,2023
Once I first saw entered the building on the first day of the Red Hat TY program, I did not know what to expect, but upon being greeted by the woman at reception, I was sent to a desk where we were given a laptop, a keycard and a bag of various gifts such as a water bottle and a speaker.

We were then escorted out into a conference room, where we were intoduced to some members of staff, and told eachother what schools we were from, after this we were given icebreaker games to do, such as one where each of us had a famous persons name stuck to our back and we had to ask people questions on who we had, we then were given a roleplay to do about how Red Hat makes its money

You can use class="has-text-centered" to highlight text like this

We were then given a tour of the building, and allowed to have our lunch. Upon us finishing lunch, we had returned to the conference room and we were given a talk on how to use the command line on linux, and how to use git and github, we were then tasked with creating a blog on how our week at Red Hat went with us needing to update it daily in accordance with any events or workshops that take place over our time. 

Day 2
On day two we were given time at the beggining of the day to be able to continue work on our blogs as we had to make more progress on them to be able to complete them, after spending a while on the graphic design of this blog, we were given a talk on the agile work process, where it was shown to us how to work efficiently in a team and how to constantly improve our skills.
